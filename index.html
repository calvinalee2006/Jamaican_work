Earn your pride

The point I am trying to make is that life can stress you out and take you down. When life does that, you find it hard to find the strength to keep going, and somtimes that can take a personal toll on you. Some people produce just enough energy to continue the monotony of life, and never feel good about themselves for any part of the day.
