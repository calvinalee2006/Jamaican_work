Growing up

The saying "You will understand when you are older" is a saying that has constantly bit me in the ass! I can not wait for the day I get to use that saying to my kids. I have the father sigh, look, tone, and stance ready for both of my children, and both of them will recieve that saying different ways, in different scenarios, but it will mean the same thing word for word just as my parents said it to me. "You will understand when you are older."
